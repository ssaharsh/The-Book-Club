ABOUT US

Reading is the process of taking in the sense or meaning of letters, symbols, etc., especially by sight or touch. For educators and researchers, reading is a multifaceted process involving such areas as word recognition, orthography (spelling), alphabetics, phonics, phonemic awareness, vocabulary, comprehension, fluency, and motivation. People who read at least seven books a year are reported to be 2.3 times more intelligent than people who read only one. And the people who read the book are much more confident than non readers while expressing themselves in front of others. As we all know books can give us a new perspective to see the world and learn from it, and who knows it will give us a new way to live our life, discover things that even we didn’t know we were capable of, but this is possible only when one starts to read a book. Due to the pandemic the world has shifted towards technology, because of which in the age of making friends, exploring new places, meeting other people, people are getting so addicted to mobiles and computers that they are not even aware of what is happening out there in the world. Let’s assume that a person starts to read a book, but what is the guarantee he or she will not lose interest in the book, or due to some interruption they might lose the flow of reading the book and never be able to continue reading it. It is said that Rome was not built in a day. Similarly, interest in reading does not grow in one day; it takes time and constant reading of books. So with this project we are promoting reading and developing lifelong reading habits, and this book club provides you with books at low cost so that money does not become a barrier in gaining knowledge. With this, we want to bring people out of the virtual world to the real world.
